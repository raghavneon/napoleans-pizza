Our Story

Parma's Homemade Pizza, Since Day One

Napoleon's Pizza has been a State Road staple for over 40 years. When Dave and Marie Lahman took over, they learned the original recipes firsthand — and they've been making them the same way ever since. No shortcuts, no chains. Just family-run pizza made from scratch.

From Dave and Marie to their sons in the kitchen, every order comes with the kind of care you only get at a place the neighborhood has trusted for decades.

What is Napoleon's Pizza known for in Parma, OH?

Napoleon's Pizza is Parma's go-to for deep dish pizza, Sicilian pizza, and Cleveland's Original Sweet Sauce. We're also known for our homemade breaded wings, specialty pizzas like the Pierogi Pizza and Wigman "58," antipasto salad, cheesy breadsticks, buffalo chicken pizza, and BBQ chicken pizza.

What's the difference between your Sicilian and Chicago deep dish?

Our Sicilian Style is a thick-crusted rectangular deep dish built like a traditional pizza, served with Cleveland's Original Sweet Sauce. Our Chicago Style is a 10" round, two-layer stuffed pie packed with a full pound of cheese and three toppings of your choice — it's a full meal on its own.
